Microbial Farming

Research Goals

There is an urgent need to reduce fertilizer application in the flower sector to attain sustainable crop production. Plant-growth promoting microbes (PGPM) are a viable alternative to traditional fertilizers for enhancing plant productivity and improving soil quality without environmental pollution.
